Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd

Dimitri is one of my favorite characters from Fire Emblem: Three Houses. As I am also a fan of the Persona series, I intended to do a series where I draw the three main characters of FE3H in the uniforms of the characters of Persona 3-5. This is the first of the drawings where Dimitri wears the Persona 3 protagonist's uniform.
