My Personal Keyboard

Date built: June 2018

This keyboard has been my faithful companion ever since I started building keyboards. Even though I've built lots of cool keyboards since then, I never got used to using them and always went back to this one. The specs are the following:

  • PCB: DZ60 from KBDFans
  • Plate: 60% Aluminum Plate (Type B) from KBDFans
  • Switches: Sakurios switches (62g Silent Linear) from ZealPC, lubed with Krytox 205g0
  • Keycaps: GMK Coral
  • Case: 60% Frosted Acrylic case from KBDFans

Commissioned Keyboard

Date built: April 2021

This is a keyboard that my friend commissioned me to make for her boyfriend. It was a fun experience because I got to share a hobby that I don't normally get to talk about with others. The end product looks and feels amazing, and makes me want to update my own keyboard!

  • PCB: KBD67 from KBDFans
  • Plate: 65% Aluminum Plate from KBDFans
  • Switches: NK_ Sherbet switches (Clicky) from NovelKeys, lubed with Tribosys 3203
  • Keycaps: Night Runner clones
  • Case: Custom stacked acrylic case by Ramon Imbao
